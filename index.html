THEMATIC AREAS

Health Projects

ICAN is committed to improving community health through comprehensive education programs and accessible healthcare services. From health education workshops to partnerships with healthcare providers, we strive to promote wellbeing and prevent diseases.

Environmental Conservation

Our conservation initiatives focus on sustainable practices like tree planting and environmental education. By safeguarding natural resources, we aim to ensure a healthy environment for future generations

Community Empowerment

Through project formulation and implementation, ICAN supports economic development and social cohesion. We empower individuals with skills training, income-generating projects, and awareness campaigns to foster self-sufficiency and resilience

Our Values

Inclusivity

The organization aims to be open and accessible to all eligible individuals residing within the community, fostering a commitment to inclusivity and community engagement.

Diversity

ICAN recognizes the importance of diversity in its activities and encourages participation from members of different backgrounds.

Equity

The organization strives to ensure fairness and equal opportunities for all members in its decision-making processes and activities.

Access

ICAN aims to provide diverse resources that promote self-sufficiency and foster community and economic development.
