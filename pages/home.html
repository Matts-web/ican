Welcome to I-CAN

INUANA COMMUNITY ACTION NETWORK(I CAN)

OUR Vision: To create a healthier, more prosperous and empowered rural community in Kenya.

Thematic Areas

Dedication to inclusivity, diversity, equity, and access define our approach to crafting programs and interventions that meet the needs of all community members.

Youth Empowerment

Creating avenues for meaningful youth engagements in leadership programs and advocating for gender equality to shape better futures.

Climate Care

Promoting environmental awareness and action through initiatives focusing on climate change adaptation and natural resource management.

Health Aid

Providing resources and programs that boost health and well-being, highlighting HIV awareness, maternal health, and disease prevention.

Community Build

Building a strong community foundation by facilitating connections, sharing knowledge, and empowering local leaders and organizations.

Equity Focus

We work to bridge gaps and create equitable opportunities for under-represented groups, ensuring fair access to services and advantages.

Innovative Solutions

Harnessing creativity and local knowledge to develop sustainable solutions to the economic and social challenges faced by the community.

Menstrual Hygiene

Through the collective efforts of volunteers and donors, I CAN delivered a wide array of donations, including food items, sanitary pads, clothes, and school supplies.

Green Action

We're committed to sustainability and environmental stewardship as key components of community well-being and development.

Our Values

Inclusivity

The organization aims to be open and accessible to all eligible individuals residing within the community, fostering a commitment to inclusivity and community engagement.

Diversity

ICAN recognizes the importance of diversity in its activities and encourages participation from members of different backgrounds.

Equity

The organization strives to ensure fairness and equal opportunities for all members in its decision-making processes and activities.

Access

ICAN aims to provide diverse resources that promote self-sufficiency and foster community and economic development.
